M_STEPRv1 - UW HCDE439 FINAL - DEREPSZ 2020



Shoutout to 3D printers, 808s and the UW HCDE dept for inspiring me to take on this endeavor.

M_STEPRv1 is an Arduino based sound maker utilizing step-sequencer type architecture and electro-mechanical noise generation brought to you be a stepper motor. Utilizing many learnings from the HCDE 439 Physical Computing class taught by Nadya Peek and assisted by TA Jasper Tran O'Leary, a prototype instrument has been developed and created from scratch without any prior microcontroller experience.

Many challenges were faced in the development of this device. Including the world catching fire. Please hold.
